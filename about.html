About us

Purpose:
Trailer Hunt is a platform created to provide trailers of movies from various genres and languages. It aims to be a comprehensive resource for movie enthusiasts to watch trailers and get a glimpse of upcoming and popular films.
Key Points:
  • Trailers from different genres including action, comedy, drama, horror, sci-fi, and more.

  • Easy navigation to search for specific movie trailers.

  • Available as a website and potentially a mobile app for both iOS and Android users.

  • Integration with social media platforms to share trailers with friends and followers.

  • Casual viewers interested in discovering new movies through trailers.

vision and mission:

Vision : To become the go-to platform for movie trailers, offering an unparalleled user experience.

Mission : To provide movie enthusiasts with easy access to a vast collection of trailers, fostering a community of informed and engaged viewers.
